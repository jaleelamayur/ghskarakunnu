About Us

G.H.S.S. Karakunnu is a well-established government school located in Malappuram District, Kerala. The school has a long-standing tradition of excellence in academics, co-curricular activities, and holistic student development.

Academics

We offer classes from 1st standard to 12th standard, under the Kerala State Education Board. Our experienced faculty ensures a strong academic foundation and encourages critical thinking and creativity in our students.

Facilities

  • Spacious Classrooms
  • Science Laboratory
  • Computer Lab
  • Library with a wide collection of books
  • Playground and sports equipment
